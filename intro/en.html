The Charité CovAPP:

  • individual risk assessment
  • recommendations for your actions and contact information
  • decision support for doctor’s visit and Corona testing
  • information to reduce your risk of infection

Anonymous, fast, free and developed by Charité physicians.


What is the CovAPP?

The CovAPP is a software developed by Charité. You can use it to assess your personal risk of suffering from coronavirus within minutes and receive helpful information. The program asks you a series of questions to assess the risk and provide recommendations for action as well as point out relevant contact points.

In this way, the symptom checker can help you to better asses your medical condition, provide recommendations regarding doctor’s visits or Corona testing and summarize relevant medical information for future doctor’s consultation.


How can I use CovAPP?


1. Start the survey by clicking „Start Questionnaire“. 
The survey contains questions regarding your current symptoms, travel and contact history. This information is also relevant for a doctor’s visit. Answering the survey will take approximately 10 min. 

2. Risk assessment and recommendations.
At the end of the survey the CovAPP will assess your risk regarding a Covid-19 infection and give you a recommendation for action, for example:

  • Advice to contact the public hotline for Covid-19
  • Advice to contact your general practicioner
  • Domestic isolation

3. Preparation of doctor’s visit 
Please save your QR-Code. When given the advice for a doctor’s visit or a visit to one of the Corona-testing sites please bring the QR-Code or print the summary presented. Some Corona-testing-sites (like the Charité) can scan the code so that your answers will be made directly available to our medical staff and can be used for your consultation. This helps to fasten the process of the medical interview. If visiting your local physician or any other Corona-testing-site, please print the summary provided and bring it along to your consultation.


Which data will be transmitted through the internet?

The survey is evaluated anonymously, and all information will be processed on your smartphone. None of your answers will be forwarded to us, our server or any other third party.


When in doubt, see a physician!

The use of this app does not replace medical treatment. If you are currently feeling seriously ill, please seek medical treatment immediately.
