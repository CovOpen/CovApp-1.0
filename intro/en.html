What is CovAPP?

CovAPP is a Charité developed software which allows you to determine your current risk of a Coronavirus infection and provides useful tips. To achieve this the software asks a series of questions, creates a risk assessment for Coronavirus infection and refers to relevant contact points. Via links you can go directly to the detailed information of the Charité and the Robert-Koch-Instituts with regards to hygiene and infection prevention.
This way the symptom checker can help you to better estimate your health risk and summarize all relevant medical information for your physician.

Anamnesis data to go

At the end of the questionnaire you receive a QR code which, in case of a medical consultation in the Coronavirus ambulance of the Charité Campus Virchow Klinikum, can be read. For the consultation in the Coronavirus ambulance, please bring either your smartphone with the QR code or a printed version. In the ambulance the code will be scanned to immediately share the answers from the questionnaire with the medical personnel.
For visits at another Coronavirus ambulance, please download and print the textual summary and bring it to your medical consultation.

In case of doubt, go and see a physician

The usage of this app does not replace medical attendance. If you feel seriously ill, please seek medical attention immediately.
