What is CovAPP?

With the CoVAPP you’ll help medical doctors by summarizing the medical history and relevant medical information regarding Covid-19.

History record ready to go

At the end of the survey, you will receive a QR code an overview of you answers.

If you are visiting the Corona outpatient clinic of the Charité Campus Virchow, we only need this QR- code to directly make your answers available to our medical staff for the doctor's consultation. You will still have an interview with a clinician.

If you visit a different Corona outpatient clinic in Berlin, please print out the overview of the answers shown on your smartphone or show it to the clinician.

If in doubt, see a doctor!

The use of this app does not qualify as medical treatment. If you currently feel seriously ill, please seek medical help.
